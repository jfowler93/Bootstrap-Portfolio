About Me

My name is Joshua Fowler. I am 26 years old and I live in Downtown Orlando, Florida. I graduated UCF in 2015 with a bachelor's degree in human communications. With that degree I worked in radio for 5 years and continue to dabble while I bartend and go to school. I am currently enrolled in UCF coding bootcamp in order to learn a new skill set and enter a new career field.

I have been married to my wife for just 4 months now. We have two beautiful cats together. We also still have a roommate, which is similar to having a child. Together we enjoy travelling, trying new foods and cocktails, and going to shows.

Working in radio has afforded me some cool opportunities. For example, I once shared a stage with Coolio and Joey Fatone. However, nothing compares to the sense of accomplishment felt when your nephews are marginally impressed by your collection of Fortnite skins.
